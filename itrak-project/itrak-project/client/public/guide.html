I-TRAK
Add to Home Screen Guide

Install I-TRAK on your iPhone in under a minute.
No App Store required — just follow these 5 steps.

1
🧭 Open Safari
On your iPhone, open Safari and go to:
superstack-ai-app.vercel.app
2
⬆️ Tap the Share Button
Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the Safari screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
3
➕ Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the Share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
✅ Confirm the App Name
The name will show as "I-TRAK". Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
5
🚀 Launch I-TRAK
Tap the I-TRAK icon on your home screen. It opens in full-screen mode, just like a native app.
💡 Tip
I-TRAK works offline once installed and syncs your workouts and meals automatically when you reconnect. Sign in with Google to keep your data in sync across all your devices.
